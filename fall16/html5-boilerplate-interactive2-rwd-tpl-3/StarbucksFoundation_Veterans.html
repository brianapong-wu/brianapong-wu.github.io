YOUTH

At Starbucks we believe that honoring our veterans and military spouses requires more than saying “Thank you.” It’s about building meaningful relationships, staying connected, and providing opportunities for veterans and their families. These men and women are making our company better. Recognizing that service members are invaluable to the success of our business, Starbucks committed to hiring 10,000 veterans and military spouses by 2018. To date we have hired more than 6,500, and we are just getting started.
